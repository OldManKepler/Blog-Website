Entry

"Why do I need to be early to wash dishes? No dirty dishes come in until about 30 minutes after opening."
"Maybe it's about principle. The van with the ingredients arrives hours before the chefs get here."
"That's not the same. It's important to know that the shop will have everything it needs."
"It doesn't need clean dishes?"
"Not right away."
The kitchen was layed out like a cross. Except one of its prongs had been cut and a foggy window layed across, creating a really long wall between the brakery station and fridge. In the middle was an island of clean anodized metal tables. The whole place was lit like a hospital operation room. Shiny pots, pans and plates reflected the diffuse lighting into everyones eyes.
"She said if I'm late again she'll find another dish washer."
"Do you want another job? This could be your big break."
"I want another boss."
"Even after she made you that schedule? She laminated it. I saw it. All the different colours."
