Loved Treasured Regardless

Entry

I'm writing this on tuesday actually so... yesterday was a day of creation and destruction. On the one hand, it was my first time driving in brazil; on the other hand, I drove over a pothole and got a flat tyre. But the universe is still balanced since I put a new string onto the guitar. This guitar, which once belonged to a cousin, is going to be my main chordophone instrument which I will use whilst I'm here. It's already heavy with memories built in - I have the opportunity to add some more.
