"What do you want to be when you go older?"
"When I grow up I would like to be... an explorer. I would like to explore in the trees and climb as high as I could get. Maybe I'd find some dinosaur bones at the top of some trees."
"Like Indiana Jones?"
"No, not like Indiana Jones. Just like myself."
"And who's that?"
"Whoever you want it to be. Trying to keep it as vague as possible. This is too tricky."
